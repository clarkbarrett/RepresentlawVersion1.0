Family, Children & Partnerships

Matrimonial and family issues can be a great cause of worry so it pays to have a skilful sympathetic lawyer working for you. Even if you are living together, in any partnership it is important to record your arrangements as this may save a great deal of anxiety and expense later on. We have a dedicated team committed to providing clear and proportionate advice. We understand the emotional and financial difficulties people face when a relationship breaks down. We know this is a worrying and stressful time and is particularly difficult if children are involved.

We can assist with a full range of family matters such as:

  • Divorce and Separation
  • Financial provision including complex issues relating to businesses, pensions and maintenance
  • Civil Partnerships
  • Conciliation
  • Mediation
  • Prenuptial / Postnuptial agreements
  • Domestic Violence
  • Injunctive proceedings eg non-molestation/occupation orders
  • Maintenance
  • Issues involving children including Parental Responsibility, Child Arrangement orders, formally known as residence & Contact orders
