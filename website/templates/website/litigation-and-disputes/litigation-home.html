Litigation and Disputes

We can guide and represent you on all matters relating to disputes leading towards a hearing if not an early and fair settlement. With high levels of experience in dealing with High Court and County Court proceedings we can help you if you have a problem concerning commercial, business and personal matters. We can advise early on in the case on the prospects of success and the weaknesses in any case and advise you on taking a commercially minded and cost effective approach to resolving any dispute and represent you towards securing a fair and reasonable compromise.

By way of an example, we can assist you with:

  • Contractual Claims and building disputes.
  • Disputed Wills and estates.
  • Breach of Contract Terms and Misrepresentation.
  • Recovery of Unpaid Debts and Enforcement Issues.
  • Partnership and Business Disputes.
  • Professional Negligence Claims.
